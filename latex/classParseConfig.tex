Converts raw configuration lines into a sequence of tokens. 

Iterates through the given map of line numbers to lexemes and determines the type of each lexeme based on predefined keywords. Unknown lexemes are marked as UNKNOWN, and lexemes following a semicolon or comment are treated as comments.
